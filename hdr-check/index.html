HDR support check

This quick page helps us see whether your browser shows real HDR photos correctly. It runs entirely on your device — nothing is uploaded. Takes about 15 seconds.

Step 1 · Look

Do these two photos look different?

If your screen shows HDR, photo A should look noticeably brighter / more vivid — its highlights (sky, water glints) should glow. If A and B look the same, HDR isn’t showing.
