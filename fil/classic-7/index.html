Classic 7 Photo Viewer

Classic 7 Photo Viewer brings back the fast, no-nonsense Windows Photo Viewer you remember — as a proper "Open with" choice on Windows 11.

No registry hacks, no ads — completely free

The classic viewer never left your PC; Windows just stopped letting you pick it. This free app puts it back, registering itself as a file-type handler that opens your images in the built-in Windows Photo Viewer — no registry edits, no fuss.

Simple and free

  • One-click Open Image, or right-click any photo → Open with → Classic 7 Photo Viewer
  • Set it as your default for any image type in Settings → Default apps
  • Fast, clean, distraction-free viewing — the way it used to be
  • Completely free, no ads

Opens what Windows already knows

  • JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, ICO
  • JPEG XR (JXR, WDP, HDP)
  • HEIC/HEIF, WebP, AVIF (with the matching free Store codec)
  • RAW camera files (with Microsoft's free Raw Image Extension)
